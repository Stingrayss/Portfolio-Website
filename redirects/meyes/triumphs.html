Triumphs

Challenges

Here are some of the challenges faced by Meyes:

  • Technology not working in low light situations
  • Technology was prone to giving inaccurate scans or movements

Since this is a webcam it requires the room to be lit. If it isn't it becomes harder for it to make accurate movements since it can't see your eyes. Despite the challenges we believe that the innovation's triumphs would outshine the downsides of it. The user would be able to access all the information more independently and certainly more easily than before.

Triumphs

We would like our product to help as many people as possible in the future and to be able to think out an assistive technology like this is an achievement in itself. We would like to thank you for visiting our website and taking time to read about our innovation.
