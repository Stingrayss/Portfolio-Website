Our Goal

Mission

Our mission is to provide a technology for those with little to no movement of their hands to be able to be able to freely use their computers again worry-free. Some need a more relaxing experience while using their computer, and this technology can benefit them as well. Instead of needing to get up to grab a remote, you can instead use your eyes as one. Assistive technology for people with restricted movement of their limbs has come a long way, nevertheless there is still a lot to be made when it comes to helping them use computers and other smart devices.

Who we would like to help

As before mentioned, our goal is to help users get a better experience when using their devices. We hope people that suffer from Parkinson's disease, Carpal Tunnel Syndrome, and people who have lost movement of their arms can benefit from this product.
There are around 10 million people worldwide living with Parkinson's disease according to the Parkinson's Foundation website and 2.7% of the general population suffer from CTS according to My Virtual Medical Centre. Those are a big part of the population, and they deserve a more independent way to access the information that the internet provides. We understand that some might have trouble using the technology and they might require assistance from time to time, but the learning curve is not too sharp and we believe that everyone can learn and benefit from it.
