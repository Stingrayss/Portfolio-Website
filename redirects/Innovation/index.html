Becoming Even More Lazy

-

AI-based robots have been becoming more and more advanced in the past decade, and it's finally time that they become more focused on human-assisting interaction.

Their purpose is simple: perform household tasks that would otherwise be performed by a human.

Things such as pouring coffee, laying out clothes, doing the dishes, etc. are all household tasks that may be performed by an assisting robot in the home.
