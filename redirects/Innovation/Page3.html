The Purpose

-

AI-based robots are not just for laziness, but can help out business and the needy.

So who does this benefit? What is the need for an AI-robot? It's quite simple really; it helps everyone. However, more specifically, this type of invention is for the needy and businesses.

The needy can use these robots in many ways. A humanoid robot creates a companion who knows how to deal with whatever issue a person may have. They can also perform the tasks that the user no longer can. This could be:

  • Reaching a highshelf due to not being able to stand up or walk
  • Preparing a meal/feeding the user for someone who can't use arms or hands
  • Being a guardian for a child while the parent is away
  • Being a teach for a child without much parent presence
